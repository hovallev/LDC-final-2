# BICECORP 2024 Integrated Report – Detailed English‑Language Summary

---
## 1  Executive Summary
2024 was the most transformative year in **BICECORP**’s 46‑year history. The company delivered solid operating results while launching the full integration with **Grupo Security (GS)**. Once completed, the merger will create one of the three‑largest private financial groups in Chile with **> USD 34 billion** in consolidated assets. Meanwhile, BICECORP deepened its customer‑centric digital strategy, expanded internationally and accelerated its ESG agenda.

### Key 2024 highlights
- **Merger progress** – promise agreement signed *Apr 2024*; all regulatory approvals obtained by *Oct 2024*; takeover bid *(OPA)* in *Jan–Feb 2025* secured **98.43 %** of GS shares.
- **Financials** – CLP 2.27 trn revenue (+1.6 %); CLP 202.9 bn net profit (–10.8 %); ROE 14.3 %; **AA/AA+** ratings across entities.
- **Customers & culture** – Bank NPS **+40 %**; BICE Vida NPS **70 %** in annuities; > 700 employees now operate in agile squads.
- **Digital & innovation** – 4 m **BICE Connect** API transfers; **AIDES AI** cut credit‑analysis time 80 %; Mercado Pago daily‑yield account hit 1.4 m clients & CLP 419 bn AUM.
- **ESG** – *Huella Chile* GHG certification; IFC **USD 200 m** women‑focused mortgage line; BICE Vida joined **Diálogos por el Clima LatAm**.
